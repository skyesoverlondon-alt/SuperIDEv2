Wave 2 • Music Workstation Suite

Five more browser-native music MVPs: looper, bassline machine, sample chopper, clip launcher, and vocal stack planner. Same family lane as the synth app, just with more little monsters in the room.

Loop Forge

Scene looper with per-lane lengths, reverse, half-speed, and motion control.

Bass Lab

Mono bass synth sequencer with glide, accent, scale lock, and mutate tools.

Sample Chop Station

Load audio, slice it, map pads, and sequence chop patterns.

Performance Pad Deck

Clip-launch surface with deck blending, stutter, risers, and quick scene logic.

Vocal Stack Builder

Choir-ish preview plus section-based harmony arrangement and notes.

What changed

This wave leans more production workflow than single-instrument play. It moves from “one cool toy” into “small weird toolkit.”

Offline-friendly

Every page includes explicit file-path navigation so the suite still hops around cleanly from a local ZIP review before deployment.

Browser goblin note: tap Power / Audio once before playback if Chrome wants a ritual gesture.
