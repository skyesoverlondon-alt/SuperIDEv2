Doctor Ops Wave II

Thirteen more doctor-focused operational apps.

This wave is aimed at actual clinic throughput, follow-up recovery, revenue friction, credentialing, preventive care, risk tracking, surgical readiness, equipment fulfillment, transitional care, and quality measure execution. Static. Offline-first. Local browser storage. Synthetic data only. No fake “one page and vibes” nonsense.

What is in the box

Every app in this wave has its own page, its own storage key, its own form schema, its own seed data, its own saved records, its own filters, its own audit trail, JSON import/export, CSV export, and clone/edit/delete behavior. These are meant as real control surfaces you can open locally and extend later.

Use synthetic data only in this build. This version does not connect to EHR systems, payers, or real patient records.
