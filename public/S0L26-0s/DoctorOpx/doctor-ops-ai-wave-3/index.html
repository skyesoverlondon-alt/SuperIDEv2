Doctor Ops AI Wave III

Thirteen more doctor-focused operational apps, this time with real browser-side AI drafting.

This wave adds AI-oriented tools for differential drafting, inbox triage, chart synthesis, prior auth, referrals, patient instructions, coding review, risk stratification, outreach writing, result explanation, chronic care planning, surgical prep, and quality-gap narratives. Static. Offline-first. Local browser storage. Synthetic data only. No fake “one page and wishful thinking” nonsense.

What is actually inside

Every app has its own page, record schema, seed data, CRUD workflow, search, filters, local persistence, JSON import/export, CSV export, audit log, and an AI Copilot panel that generates structured drafts from selected records or live form data. The AI layer runs in-browser and produces summaries, triage recommendations, outreach scripts, medical-necessity drafts, coding clues, and more.

Synthetic demo only. These are operational drafting tools, not autonomous clinical decision makers. A human clinician remains the actual wizard with a license.
