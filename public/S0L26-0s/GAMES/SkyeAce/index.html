Battle Journal

How SkyeAce Works

SkyeAce keeps the core bones of spades: four seats, partner teams, bids, tricks, and spades as trump. Then it adds battle pressure with health bars and level progression.

  • South + North are one team. West + East are the other.
  • Bid before the hand starts. Nil is allowed.
  • You must follow suit if you can.
  • Spades are trump and cannot be led until broken, unless you only have spades.
  • Make your team bid to score. Miss it and you get smacked with a negative round.
  • The stronger round also deals damage to the other team.
  • First team to 150 score or to drop the other team to 0 HP wins.
