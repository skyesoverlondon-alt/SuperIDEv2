How To Position The Launcher

  1. Sell the suite as the umbrella launcher for the kAIxU universe-building product line, not as a generic app directory.
  2. Lead with one of the four platform tracks depending on the buyer: Genesis Studio, World Engine, Mythic Intelligence, or Command Deck.
  3. Use Matrix and Persona when the buyer needs incubation or cast design positioned as explicit products instead of hidden support tools.
  4. Use Neural Space Pro as the executive intelligence layer that routes between the kAIxU platforms without dropping back to the full SuperIDE shell.
  5. Keep this launcher as the branded front door whenever you want kAIxU to feel like its own platform inside the larger ecosystem.
Architecture

One Ecosystem, Separate Front Door

This page does not split the runtime. It gives kAIxU its own market-ready front door while keeping every app on the same shared SuperIDE foundation, auth model, workspace context, and gateway layer.
